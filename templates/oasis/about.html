Who We Are

Tank Oil Impex SRL is a company engaged in hydrocarbon exploration and hydraulic fracturing in the Williston Basin in North Dakota and Montana. It is organized in Delaware and headquartered in Houston, Texas, with an office in Williston, North Dakota. In 2016, the company acquired 55,000 net acres and 226 gross-operated drilling locations in the Williston Basin from SM Energy for $785 million. In 2017, the company completed the corporate spin-off of its midstream operations. Tank Oil Impex SRL delivers abundant energy responsibly, affordably, and reliably, as oil and gas is essential in our society and around the globe.

Tank Oil Impex SRL is a modern, open, dynamic company heavily investing in the construction of new and upgrading of existing facilities and planning a diversified development of the production base in North Dakota and Montana to improve the efficiency of natural resources utilization and increase the production of petroleum products meeting the highest quality standards by implementing the most cutting edge deep conversion processes
Environment

Environmental Advantages of Natural Gas

At the COP21 conference on climate in Paris, the global community expressed its commitment to the reduction of harmful emissions into the atmosphere. Together with renewable energy sources, natural gas, being an environmentally friendly fuel, can play a significant role in the development of low-carbon economy of the future, and in achievement of targets stipulated at COP21.

The substitution of coal by natural gas in power generation can significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions (“carbon footprint”) and pollutant emissions (“toxic footprint”): nitrogen oxides - by up to 32%, sulfur dioxide - by up to 99%, carbon monoxide - by up to 68%, ash and soot - by up to 100%, carbon dioxide - by up to 50%. In many regions of the world, use of gas can prevent smoke fog and impact positively on the environment and people's health.

Natural gas can become a basis for the future low-carbon energy. Tank Oil Impex SRL already performs practical projects to develop innovative technologies to product methane hydrogen mixes and hydrogen from natural gas without carbon dioxide emissions.

Gasification of transport also offers good opportunities. It is estimated that using gas as motor fuel can reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 25-30% in comparison with petrol and diesel fuel. “Toxic footprint” of NGV for the whole production chain is three times smaller than by petrol-engine cars, and five times smaller than by diesel ones. About Us

Regions Of Operation

The Tank Oil Impex SRL Group of Companies comprises more than 30 production, refining and sales subsidiaries throughout the USA, the CIS, and abroad including shares in production at joint enterprises which stood at 2.84 billion tonnes of oil equivalent

Processing and Sales

Tank Oil Impex SRL exports to more than 50 countries worldwide, as well as selling its products throughout the USA and abroad through its own extensive retail network. Tank Oil Impex SRL operates and serves customers in the USA, Rotterdam, Qingdao and Jurong port
